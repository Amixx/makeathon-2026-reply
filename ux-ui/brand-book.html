01 · Essence

A coach, not a chatbot.

Three minutes to share where you're going. Five concrete things to do this month. A check-in next month so you don't drift.

01 · Promise

We stay with you.

ChatGPT waits to be asked. LinkedIn waits to be searched. wayTUM comes back next Sunday to ask what actually happened — and adjusts the plan.

02 · Method

Vision first, CV later.

Start with who you want to become. Five agents — study, career, campus, extracurricular, scholarship — orchestrate a monthly plan grounded in real data.

03 · Posture

European by design.

EU-hosted. Voice audio deleted within 24 hours. Recommendations framed as exploration, never scoring. Not ad-funded, not US-centric — and not apologising for it.

01 · METADATA — SYSTEM LABEL
/ JetBrains Mono
Why one family? Mixing display and text types invites visual noise — and Copilot's whole posture is "less, sharper." Inter at weight 800 with -0.045 letter-spacing gives us the tight, quietly confident display that Linear and Superhuman get away with. JetBrains Mono is a signal, not a style — it marks system moments (labels, keys, timestamps, metadata) so when it appears, the user knows it means something specific.

07 · Trust Signals

EU-native by design.

Trust is not a trust badge — it's a set of decisions. These are ours.

EU

Hosted in the EU. Always.

Frankfurt region. No US subprocessors without SCCs + Transfer Impact Assessment on file.

AI

AI Act by choice, not exception.

We frame recommendations as exploration, never scoring — staying clear of Annex III high-risk. No "85% match." Ever.

24h

Voice deleted within 24 hours.

We keep the transcript. We never keep the audio. Biometric data is not ours to store.

∅

Not ad-funded. Ever.

Students use us free via their university. Universities pay the per-seat fee. We never sell data and never run ads.

Σ

Outcomes, not opinions.

We publish retention, application-completion, and award-rate data with every university pilot. Accountability is the feature.

OA

OAuth, not scraping.

LinkedIn, GitHub, TUM SSO — every integration is user-mediated. We don't touch data the user didn't hand us.
